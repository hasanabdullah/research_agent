## Evaluation Criteria (to be applied in Cycles 3-4)

Each opportunity will be scored 1-5 on:

1. **Revenue potential** — Can it realistically reach $10K/mo? $50K/mo? $100K/mo?
2. **Startup cost** — Total cash needed before first revenue
3. **Time to revenue** — Months from start to first $1K, then to $10K
4. **Competition level** — How crowded is the niche?
5. **Skill requirements** — What expertise is needed? How rare is it?
6. **Scalability** — Can it grow beyond $10K/mo without proportional effort?
7. **Defensibility** — What prevents a competitor from copying you?
8. **Risk factors** — Churn, platform dependency, regulatory, ethical
