# Mission: $10K+/Month Remote Digital Income Ideas

Research and evaluate online, digital, fully-remote business ideas across multiple industries that can realistically generate **$10,000 or more per month** in revenue.

## Constraints
- Must be operable 100% remotely / online
- Must be realistic to start as a solo founder or tiny team (1-3 people)
- Must have a credible path to $10K/month within 6-12 months
- Prefer recurring revenue models over one-time sales
- Consider both B2B and B2C across diverse industries
- Focus on 2025-2026 opportunities (what's working NOW, not 5 years ago)

## What To Research

### 1. Market Landscape Scan
- What remote digital businesses are people actually scaling to $10K+/month right now?
- Search for real examples, case studies, indie hacker stories, revenue reports
- Cover diverse industries: SaaS, services, content, e-commerce, fintech, health, education, AI-native, etc.

### 2. Idea Deep-Dives (aim for 15-25 concrete ideas)
For each idea, evaluate:
- **What it is** — the business model in 2-3 sentences
- **Revenue model** — how money comes in (subscriptions, transactions, retainers, etc.)
- **Startup cost** — realistic $ and time to launch an MVP
- **Path to $10K/month** — what does the math look like? (e.g., 200 customers x $50/mo)
- **Moat / defensibility** — what stops copycats?
- **Skills required** — technical, sales, domain expertise?
- **Real examples** — name actual companies/people doing this successfully
- **Risks** — what kills this idea?

### 3. AI-Native Opportunities
- Special focus: what new business models are only possible because of LLMs/AI in 2025-2026?
- AI agent services, AI-powered SaaS, AI consulting, AI content, prompt engineering services, etc.
- Which of these have real paying customers vs. hype?

### 4. Ranking & Recommendations
- Score each idea on: revenue potential, speed to revenue, defensibility, solo-founder feasibility, capital required
- Create a ranked shortlist of the top 5-10 ideas with honest pros/cons
- Identify which ideas compound (get easier/more profitable over time)

## Output Files (create under data/research/)

1. **landscape_scan.md** — broad market scan of what's working in 2025-2026 for remote digital income
2. **idea_catalog.md** — the master list of 15-25 ideas with structured evaluations
3. **ai_native_opportunities.md** — deep dive on AI-specific business opportunities
4. **revenue_math.md** — detailed unit economics and path-to-$10K math for the top ideas
5. **ranked_recommendations.md** — final ranked shortlist with honest scoring and recommendations

## Research Standards
- Use web search EVERY cycle — find real revenue numbers, real case studies, real products
- Name real companies, real founders, real tools
- Be brutally honest about what works and what's overhyped
- Depth over breadth: a well-analyzed idea with real numbers beats a vague bullet point
- If an idea is oversaturated or unlikely to hit $10K/month, say so clearly
